Dark Chocolate

This type of chocolate contains a higher percentage of cocoa solids and a lower amount of sugar compared to other types. It typically has a more intense and slightly bitter flavor profile. Dark chocolate is known for its potential health benefits due to its higher cocoa content.

Milk Chocolate

Milk chocolate is made by adding milk powder or condensed milk to the chocolate mixture. It has a creamy and sweet taste, thanks to the added dairy components. Milk chocolate is generally milder in flavor compared to dark chocolate and is a popular choice for candy bars and desserts.

White Chocolate

White chocolate is technically not a true chocolate because it does not contain cocoa solids. Instead, it is made from cocoa butter, sugar, and milk solids. It has a smooth, buttery texture and a sweet flavor with hints of vanilla. White chocolate is often used in confectionery and baking.
